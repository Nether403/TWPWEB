* **The One-Pager:**  A concise summary (≤600 words) defining the mission, the "Flawed Parent" data problem, and the founders' "why me" credibility.

#### 4\. Phase III: Technical Architecture and the Ethical Data Pipeline

The technical architecture is divided between  **"The Instrument"**  (the secure interface) and  **"The Inquisitor"**  (the AI facilitator). Together, they form an ethical data pipeline designed to extract human wisdom while maintaining absolute data sanctity.

##### The Ethical Data Pipeline: Immutable Provenance

To ensure trust with safety-conscious stakeholders, all personally identifiable information (PII) follows a strictly bolded sequence:  **Intake → Hash → Vault → De-link**

1. **Intake:**  Testimony is gathered via a minimalist, HTTPS-secured portal.  
2. **Hash:**  A  **SHA-256 content hash**  is generated to ensure data integrity.  
3. **Vault:**  Identity data is stored in a segregated, KMS-encrypted vault.  
4. **De-link:**  PII is scrubbed. The de-identified record is stored using  **IPFS Content Identifiers (CIDs)**  for decentralized storage.**The "So What?" of RFC-3161:**  We utilize  **RFC-3161 timestamps**  to provide "Before Midnight" proof-of-existence. This strategically allows us to prove we possessed the wisdom at a specific point in time without requiring a central authority or a third-party audit of our private database, creating a decentralized and immutable audit trail.
