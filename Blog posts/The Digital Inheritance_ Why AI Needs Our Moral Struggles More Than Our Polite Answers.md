Current AI training methods, such as standard RLHF, often curate data toward agreement, resulting in "lukewarm, flattened answers." The Witness Protocol argues for  **Sycophancy Resistance** . To protect the corpus from "digital noise" and data poisoning, every contribution must pass through "The Gate," a rigorous vetting process:

These prompts require a level of accountability and cognitive flexibility that cannot be faked or generated by standard AI patterns.
