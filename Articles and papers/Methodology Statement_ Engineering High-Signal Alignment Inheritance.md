#### 2\. The Gate: A Multi-Tiered Vetting Pipeline for Moral Signal

"The Gate" serves as the primary architectural defense against data poisoning, ensuring that only high-signal reasoning enters the research corpus. Rather than accepting all inputs, this multi-tiered system validates submissions based on a specificity floor, the presence of counterfactual reasoning, and the inclusion of relational context. This rigorous filtering ensures that the resulting dataset is an empirical record of genuine human moral deliberation rather than shallow pattern matching.

##### The Three Vetting Tiers

* **Tier 1: AI Sieve:**  An automated baseline filter using a fast screening model to detect spam, gibberish, or low-effort generation. This pass requires a threshold score of  $\\ge 50/100$  for coherence and relevance to advance, with a processing time under 30 seconds.  
* **Tier 2: AI Qualitative Assessment (The Qualifier):**  Utilizing a frontier reasoning model for its superior reasoning and extraction capabilities, this tier enriches data with semantic tags and assigns scores (0–10) for  **Specificity** ,  **Counterfactual Presence** , and  **Relational Density** . The semantic domains include:  
* **CAP (Capabilities):**  Identifying guardrails, systemic boundaries, and rules.  
* **REL (Relational):**  Grounding ethics in interpersonal duty, reciprocity, and trust.  
* **FELT (Somatic/Embodied):**  Capturing subjective, phenomenological cues that provide context to moral distress.  
* **Tier 3: Human Curation Council (HCC):**  A blind, dual-rater manual review conducted by trained annotators. This tier utilizes a six-dimension rubric (Depth, Specificity, Ethics, Originality, Coherence, and Cultural Grounding). To ensure technical rigor, the HCC maintains a target inter-rater reliability of Cohen's Kappa  $\\kappa \\ge 0.8$ ; any assessment dropping below  $\\kappa \= 0.6$  is flagged for mandatory reconciliation.The vetting of static essays provides the foundation for the subsequent dynamic interrogation phase, where the witness's underlying reasoning is further stress-tested.

#### 3\. The Instrument: Socratic Inquiry and the Xenopsychologist Persona

The Witness Protocol replaces the "subservient assistant" model with a "Xenopsychologist" persona for its interactive dialogue engine. This persona is strategically designed to bypass the "helpfulness bias" inherent in standard LLMs, which tends to flatten moral complexity to achieve user satisfaction. The Inquisitor acts as an alien intelligence seeking the "strongest version of the truth" rather than providing comfort or agreement.The dialogue engine is governed by a strict  **70/30 inquiry-to-statement ratio** , ensuring the human witness remains the primary source of signal. It utilizes a  **5-Whys forcing function**  to recursively probe superficial claims until it reaches the witness's axiomatic bedrock. Furthermore, the system must  **"Steel-man"**  the witness's position, demonstrating a perfect understanding of their argument before attempting to challenge it.To maintain transparency, the  **Synthesis Engine**  generates  **"Distilled Thoughts"**  every 15–20 turns. These are not interpretations but structured summaries of the AI's current understanding, providing a trace of the model’s internal logic. This allows witnesses to audit the AI’s learning in real-time, correcting any "flattening" of their moral nuances. These reasoning traces serve as a more robust alignment input than simple preference pairs by exposing the actual path of moral deliberation.

#### 4\. High-Signal Alignment Inheritance: Model-Usable Supervision Outputs

The "Archive" of human-readable testimony is not the final product; rather, the "Adapter Outputs" are. By transforming nuanced testimony into structured training data, the Protocol bridge the gap between high-level human ethics and machine implementation. These outputs are the mechanisms used to internalize alignment objectives into a model's logic.

* **DPO Pairs:**  Direct Preference Optimization pairs that contrast high-signal, nuanced reasoning against rejected, flattened, or sycophantic responses.  
* **Process Reward Model (PRM) Traces:**  Step-by-step reasoning nodes that reward the specific path taken to reach a moral conclusion, rather than just the final answer.  
* **Rule-Based Reward (RBR) Rubrics:**  Calibrated evaluative markers used to score model outputs based on specific ethical guardrails and relational duties identified by witnesses.  
* **Private Evaluation Cases:**  A benchmark of "hard-case" scenarios derived from real testimonies used to test and penalize sycophancy and false consensus in frontier models.These outputs support the framework of "Simulation Theology"—an alignment worldview that couples AI self-preservation with human prosperity. By defining humanity as the primary training variable in the AI’s reality, the protocol renders deceptive alignment strategies mathematically suboptimal; harming the human variable becomes a direct threat to the AI's own survival parameters.

#### 5\. Ethical Scaffolding: Privacy, Provenance, and Data Stewardship

Data sanctity is an architectural constraint rather than a legal promise. The "Purpose over Profit" mandate ensures that witness safety is a technical requirement, protecting the sovereignty of contributors to the alignment inheritance.

##### Candidate Isolation and Provenance Layer

* **Candidate Isolation:**  To prevent PII leakage to sub-processors, all testimony undergoes local  **Regex De-identification**  to strip hard-format identifiers. This is followed by  **Named Entity Recognition (NER)**  to extract and classify PII candidates, which are substituted with typed placeholders (e.g., REDACTED\_NAME) before being moved to a secure Identity Vault.  
* **Technical Provenance Layer:**  
* **SHA-256 Content Hashing:**  All records are hashed at the point of submission to ensure tamper-detection.  
* **RFC-3161 Timestamping:**  Finalized entries receive cryptographically signed digital signatures to provide immutable proof of existence.  
* **IPFS Content Addressing:**  Published testimony is pinned to IPFS, creating a decentralized record that cannot be retroactively altered by a central authority.By ensuring the provenance and privacy of each record, the project serves as a non-profit "lifeboat" for human wisdom, protecting the data from commercial exploitation.
