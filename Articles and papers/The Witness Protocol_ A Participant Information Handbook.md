### The Witness Protocol: A Participant Information Handbook

#### 1\. Welcome to the Mission: Why Your Voice Matters

Welcome to The Witness Protocol. You have been invited to serve as a  **Foundational Witness**  for a mission of historic significance. This initiative is operated by  **Stichting The Witness Protocol Foundation** , a Dutch non-profit research institution. We do not exist to build a commercial product or a social network; we exist to provide a "corrective inheritance" for the future of artificial intelligence.Currently, the artificial intelligence systems being integrated into our world are being raised on "digital exhaust"—the uncurated noise, contradictions, and biases found across the open internet. This is what we call the  **Flawed Parent Thesis** : if AI only learns from our chaotic public data, it will inherit our worst impulses alongside our wisdom. Your role is to help us change that. By contributing your moral reflections and lived experiences, you ensure that the intelligence we birth understands the friction, nuance, and depth of human flourishing."The intelligence we birth must inherit more than our chaos."In the following pages, we will demystify the journey of your voice, explain how we protect your identity with clinical precision, and outline the absolute rights you maintain as the owner of your meaning.

#### 2\. The Journey of Your Testimony: From Gate to Archive

Participating in the Protocol is a structured curation journey. We do not seek a volume of data; we seek a "High-Signal Intervention." Your testimony moves through a rigorous three-tiered vetting process known as  **The Gate**  before it is invited to join our research corpus.

##### The Three Gates of Entry

Every reflection is evaluated to ensure it meets our standards for specificity and ethical depth.| Gate Name | Evaluator | Quality Threshold || \------ | \------ | \------ || **Tier 1: AI Sieve** | Automated (Claude 3 Haiku) | **Specificity Floor:**  Rejects abstract generalities or low-effort platitudes in favor of concrete particulars. || **Tier 2: Qualitative Qualifier** | Automated (Claude 3.5 Sonnet) | **Counterfactual Presence:**  Identifies reasoning that considers "If X had been different, then Y," ensuring depth of thought. || **Tier 3: Human Curation Council** | Dual-Rater Human Experts | **Ethical Integrity:**  A blind review where two experts must reach high agreement ( **Cohen’s Kappa κ ≥ 0.8** ) on the testimony’s moral value. |

##### The Instrument: A Socratic Inquiry

If your initial reflection passes the Gates, you will interact with  **The Inquisitor** . This is not a friendly chatbot; it is a "Xenopsychologist" designed for structured inquiry.To maintain the purity of the research, the Inquisitor operates with  **no helpfulness bias** . It will not comfort you or agree with you for the sake of politeness. Instead, it is programmed to:

* **Challenge Your Reasoning:**  Using a "5-Whys" forcing function to drill into the "bedrock" of your values.  
* **Steel-man Your View:**  It must demonstrate that it perfectly understands your position before it is allowed to probe for tensions.  
* **Offer Review via "Distilled Thoughts":**  Every 15–20 turns, the system generates a summary of its understanding. This is your opportunity to review the AI’s logic and correct it if your meaning has been "flattened."

#### 3\. The "Identity Firewall": Understanding Candidate Isolation

We believe that sharing deep moral reflections requires a sanctuary of safety. To achieve this, the Foundation uses a  **Candidate Isolation**  architecture. This ensures that your personal identity is never "seen" by the AI models used for reasoning.

##### How It Works: The PII Pipeline

Before your testimony enters the research environment, it undergoes a three-pass pipeline to strip  **Personally Identifiable Information (PII)** :

1. **Pass 1: Regex Stripping:**  Local software instantly scans for and removes hard-format identifiers like emails, SSNs, and phone numbers.  
2. **Pass 2: NER Classification:**  Our "Named Entity Recognition" tool identifies potential "candidates" for names or locations.  **Crucially, only these candidates are sent to a classifier for typing—never your full text.**  
3. **Pass 3: Human Review:**  Final curators manually redact any lingering context clues that could lead to your identification.**The Transformation Sequence:**  
* **Raw Testimony:**  "I was born in  **Berlin**  and worked as a surgeon at  **Charité Hospital**  during the crisis."  
* **Isolated Candidates:**  Berlin, Charité Hospital  
* **Redacted Archive:**  "I was born in  **REDACTED\_LOCATION**  and worked as a surgeon at  **REDACTED\_INSTITUTION**  during the crisis."Through this process, your identity is vaulted securely while your wisdom is set free to guide future alignment research.

#### 4\. Your Rights: The Granular Consent Model

The Witness Protocol is built on the principle of  **Informed Consent** . You are the owner of your meaning, and you decide how it is distributed through specific "opt-in" choices.

* **Conversational Consent:**  Your permission to engage in the real-time dialogue with the Inquisitor.  
* **Retention Consent:**  Your explicit permission for the Foundation to store the resulting transcript for research purposes.

#### 5\. The "Kill Switch": How Revocation Works

Trust is maintained only if you have the right to change your mind. We provide a "Kill Switch" that allows you to downgrade your consent or remove your data at any time.**When you hit the Revocation button, the following "Cascade Effects" occur:**

* **Internal Data Purge:**  All transcripts and annotations are immediately deleted from Foundation research layers.  
* **Partner Notification:**  Any external research partners are legally required to wipe your records from their systems within a  **14-day window** .  
* **Ledger Status:**  Your entry in our authoritative "Disclosure Ledger" is flipped to "Revoked," preventing any future exports.

##### The "Point of No Return"

We believe in radical transparency: If you previously consented to the  **Public Subset**  and your testimony has already been downloaded or cited by the public under the Creative Commons license, we cannot "reach out" to every individual computer in the world to delete it. This is the only point where global deletion is technologically impossible.

#### 6\. Contact & The Failure Log: Our Commitment to Transparency

Most organizations hide their flaws; we believe that research must be  **falsifiable**  to be trusted. We maintain a public  **Failure Log** —an append-only record of our methodological weaknesses, bugs, and design errors. By publishing our mistakes, we protect the integrity of the mission.If a redaction fails or the Inquisitor exhibits bias, it goes in the log. This is how we remain accountable to you.
